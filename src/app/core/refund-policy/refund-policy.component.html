Refund Policy

HappyLocate follows a transparent and customer friendly refund policy to ensure a superior customer satisfaction. The refund policy varies and is different for each service:

Packers and Movers:

HappyLocate offers a hassle-free relocation experience to the customers through its network of verified service providers. In case of any cancellations the refund will be initiated as per government norms or within 7 working days of receiving a cancellation request after deducting the booking confirmation fee( INR 500 for within city relocations and INR 1000 for between city(inter-city) relocations.

Hotels:

The refund shall be processed within 15-20 working days from the date of the cancellation request, after receiving back to back refund from the concerned Vendor.

The refund amount with be subject to the deduction of the cancellation charges. Please refer individual hotel booking page for the cancellation charges.

The refund policy is subject to change from time to time. The customer is advised to visit this page and keep himself/herself updated on the same.
