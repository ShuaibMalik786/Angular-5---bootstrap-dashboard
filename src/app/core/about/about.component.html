About Us

HappyLocate

Happylocate, India’s first One Stop Relocation Platform, was conceptualized to reform the way India relocates. Relocation has mostly been a nightmare and a tough experience to many where it adds to complications when users struggle to find right vendors, face price uncertainty and ignorance on managing them.

Customer Experience has been our prime focus and therefore we empanelled only Original, Reputed, established and reliable Packers Movers on the platform. No duplicates on HappyLocate. Packaging guidelines, Quote comparison and automation infused at each stage of relocation while coordinating end to end movement are the reasons why relocation should be experienced with HappyLocate.

We also proudly own an Enterprise Solution (A customizable Technology) for Corporates to help them manage end to end Employee’s Relocation. Our endeavour is to significantly improve the relocation experience through transparency, reliability and convenience.

HappyLocate Team is customer obsessed.
