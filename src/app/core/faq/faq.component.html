Insurance & Claims

Q: How do I claim in case of damage and by when do I receive my settlement amount?

Answer

It’s simple.
Step 1: Raise a request online at HappyLocate.com OR send an email at aakash.ranjan@happylocate.com sharing the inventory/goods damaged along with images of damaged goods.
Step 2: HappyLocate coordinates with the servicing Packer Mover and facilitates the settlement amount within 72 hours.

Note: Please do not sign the movement completion Receipt in case of damage identified at the time of unloading and do mention special remarks at the back of the receipt about any damage noticed at the time of unloading. Damage noticed within 48 hours of unloading can also be claimed.

Do’s and Don’ts

Q: Are there any restrictions on the type of goods and materials I can have moved?

Answer

In general, you should not pack liquids, perishable foods, flammable liquids, or any form of explosive. If you have a question about particular items please contact your Relocation Coordinator.
